Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is Japanese-style fried chicken. This recipe can also be used to make tonkatsu, a similiar dish that uses pork cutlets instead of chicken. Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce, it's one of the tastiest japanese dishes when done right. Here's what you'll need:

Ingredients

  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour..
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko..
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown..
