Lasagna

Description

Nothing beats this pasta-layer cake of saucy and cheesy goodness like good 'ol Lasagna! With cooked pasta layers, encasing bolognese and cheese sauce, you can never go wrong with a great Lasagna. Here's how to make it, and the ingredients you will need:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
